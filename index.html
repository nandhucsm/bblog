Top 5 weekend destinations near Madurai

Madurai is a happening town in Tamil Nadu with people thronging the streets throughout the day, with the buzzing markets, scrumptious food and more. Red Taxi is successfully launched in Madurai and delighting people with our comfort rides... at the most affordable rates. Our outstation rides have the most affordable packages with a promise of comfort throughout your trip. Here we have curated the top 5 weekend destination you can explore near Madurai

1. Kodaikanal
One of the most famous honeymoon destinations in India, Kodaikanal has a pleasing climate, mist- covered mountains and beautiful lakes and valleys. You are sure to enjoy the calming trip at this hill station leaving behind all your stress and unwinding here for the weekend.

2. Thekkady
Thekkady is only about 130 km away from Madurai and is a fantastic tourist spot in Kerala which houses the largest Tiger Reserve. Thekkady is a splendid choice to make if you are looking to spend a weekend in the jungle!

3. Tuticorin
Tuticorin the port city is 150 km away and has a variety of attractions including Kalakkad Wildlife Sanctuary, Kalugumalai and Tuticorin port. Pack your bags and set out to explore this remarkable city with our outstation

4. Palani
Set out on a religious trip and visit Palani, a famous pilgrim spot located very close to Madurai. It could be the perfect family outing for you for the weekend.

5. Surili Falls
Located 130 km away, you can plan a refreshing trip to Suruli falls. Nestled in a beautiful green cover, Suruli falls is a favourite picnic spot for many to enjoy and unwind. With these and more, Red taxi Outstation can provide affordable packages to several places with the promise of utmost comfort!
